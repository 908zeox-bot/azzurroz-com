The Collection

Automobiles Worth Protecting

The cars that earn a place here are the ones that carry history in their proportions — a 365, a Dino, a 330. Machines designed when engineers were still allowed to be romantics.

Azzurro Z was conceived around vehicles like these. Not as trophies. As living objects that deserve the same care and reverence they were created with.

The Origin Story

"I needed a home for my Dino 246 GTC.
What I found in Denver was climate-controlled disappointment."

My 1972 Dino 246 GTC is the most beautiful automobile I've ever sat in. Finding storage worthy of it proved harder than buying it. Generic warehouses. Indifferent staff. None of the community or craftsmanship the car represented.

So I stopped looking and started building. Azzurro Z is the facility I searched for — designed from first principles around the specific needs of classic and exotic car collectors in Denver. The name comes from the deep Italian blue that has always felt like home.

— Dave, Founder & Azzurro Z Member #1
