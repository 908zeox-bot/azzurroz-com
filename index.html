Our Origin

The Car That Started It All

It started with a single car — a 1972 Ferrari Dino 246 GTC in Azzurro blue. The color is why this place exists.

I've been collecting for most of my adult life, and for years I watched exceptional cars deteriorate in spaces never built to hold them. Underground garages with fluctuating humidity. Facilities too far from the city to visit regularly. Indifferent staff who couldn't tell a Dino from a Camry.

Azzurro Z is the facility I always wanted. Purpose-built. Climate-controlled to museum standards. Close enough to downtown that you'll actually visit. And surrounded by people who share the obsession.

We're opening Summer 2026 with twenty charter memberships. If you know, you know.

— Dave Shull, Founder
