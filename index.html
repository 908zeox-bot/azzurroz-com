Our Story

A Sanctuary Born
from Passion

Azzurro Z is a luxury vintage car storage facility in central Denver, Colorado. More than a warehouse, it's a celebration of classic car culture — named after the iconic Azzurro blue of a 1972 Dino 246 GTC, the car that inspired everything.

Located at 1495 S Platte River Drive, Denver, CO 80223, Azzurro Z is closer to downtown than any competing storage facility. Our climate-controlled warehouse provides museum-quality protection for your collection, while our community of fellow enthusiasts ensures your cars are stored among friends who share your passion.

Founded by Dave Shull, a lifelong collector who wanted a better option for storing his own vintage sports cars, Azzurro Z was built for collectors who value convenience, care, and community.

Our Services

Comprehensive Care
for Classic Cars

Beyond storage — full-service vehicle care designed for serious vintage car collectors in the Denver area.

Battery Tending & Monthly Startups

Continuous battery tending and monthly engine startups keep your classic mechanically sound and ready to drive.

Pre & Post-Trip Preparation

We prep your car before pickup — tire pressure, fluid check, warm-up — and provide a spot-free rinse when you return.

Concierge Services

From scheduling maintenance with trusted Denver mechanics to coordinating detailing — we handle logistics so you can focus on driving.

Community Events & Drives

Regular gatherings, group drives through the Colorado mountains, and exclusive experiences for Azzurro Z members.

Wash Bay Access
